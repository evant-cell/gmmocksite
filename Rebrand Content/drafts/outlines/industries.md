# Outline, /industries (Industries hub)

**Page job.** Convince a vertical buyer that GM has run their industry before, then route them to their industry page. One action: click into a vertical; secondary, request a demo.

**Page type.** Sub page structure (routing hub). Word target: 800 to 1,100.

**Keywords** (no dedicated page-plan entry; verticals carry the volume). Hub target: programmatic advertising by industry, industry-specific advertising. Each tile passes the visitor to a page targeting "[vertical] advertising" per the plan (legal 1200/KD5, retail 700/KD0, automotive 800/KD12, etc.).

**Meta title (draft).** Programmatic Advertising by Industry | Genius Monkey
**Meta description (draft, under 155 chars).** Automotive, legal, healthcare, retail, political and more. See how programmatic advertising performs in your industry, with case study numbers to match.

**Message map.** (1) Vertical context changes how campaigns should be built. (2) GM has receipts in your vertical, not generic claims. (3) Your industry page has the depth. (4) The proof lives one click away in case studies.
