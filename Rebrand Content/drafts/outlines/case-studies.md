# Outline, /programmatic-case-studies (Results / Case Studies hub)

**Page job.** The proof pillar. Let a skeptical buyer find results from a brand like theirs in under a minute, and give AI answer engines citable outcome data. One action: open a case study; secondary, request a demo.

**Page type.** Sub page structure (collection hub). Word target: 600 to 900 of copy around the collection (the case studies themselves carry the depth).

**Keywords** (from page-plan.md). Primary: programmatic advertising case studies. Secondary: programmatic advertising results, programmatic advertising roi examples. Individual studies target "[client] case study" (GM already ranks pos 2 for "canales furniture" this way).

**Meta title (draft).** Programmatic Advertising Case Studies | Genius Monkey
**Meta description (draft, under 155 chars).** Real results with real numbers. 2300% ROAS, cost per conversion from $2,000 to under $1, an 81.5% political win rate. Browse by industry.

**Message map.** (1) These are verified numbers, not marketing rounding. (2) There's a brand like yours in here. (3) The measurement behind the numbers is the same one you'd get. (4) Talk to us.
