# Outline, /about (Company hub)

**Scope note.** The Company pillar holds About, Careers, Partners, Press/News, Contact. This outline is the hub page, /about. The other four are sub-pages (P3 in the plan) and get outlined later.

**Page job.** Answer "who is Genius Monkey and why should I trust them with my budget" for buyers, press, and answer engines (this is the entity page). One action: request a demo; secondary, routes to Careers, Partners, Press, Contact.

**Page type.** Sub page structure. Brand register, triads and loud copy allowed here per Part 3. Word target: 800 to 1,100.

**Keywords** (from page-plan.md). Primary: genius monkey (branded, GM ranks #1) plus "programmatic advertising company" context. AEO: Who is Genius Monkey? E-E-A-T carrier for the whole site.

**Meta title (draft).** About Genius Monkey | Programmatic Advertising Company
**Meta description (draft, under 155 chars).** Genius Monkey is the programmatic advertising partner for brands and agencies who are done guessing. Our own DSP, real experts, transparent results.

**Message map.** (1) Who GM is, in one clean statement. (2) What GM believes and why it's built this way. (3) The people are the differentiator. (4) The client promise is structural, not a tagline. (5) Where to go next.
