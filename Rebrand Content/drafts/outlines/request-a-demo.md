# Outline, /request-a-demo (Convert)

**Page job.** Primary conversion page. Remove the last hesitation and get the form filled. One action: submit the demo request. Nothing else competes with it.

**Page type.** Sub page structure (conversion). Word target: 300 to 500. Short on purpose, every extra paragraph is a place to leave.

**Keywords** (from page-plan.md). Primary: request a demo (branded, transactional; GM already ranks for "demo monkey"). Secondary: programmatic advertising demo, see a programmatic platform demo. This page converts, it does not chase discovery traffic.

**Meta title (draft).** Request a Demo | Genius Monkey
**Meta description (draft, under 155 chars).** See the Genius Monkey platform live, your goals, your channels, your reporting. A real expert walks you through it, not a canned deck.

**Message map.** (1) The demo is about your goals, not our slideshow. (2) You'll talk to a real expert. (3) The proof says it's worth 30 minutes of your time. (4) Here's exactly what happens after you submit.
