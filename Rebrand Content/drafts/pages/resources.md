# Everything we publish, in one place

This is the programmatic advertising resource library we always wished a vendor would build. Guides, a glossary, straight answers, comparisons, research, and specs, all written by the people who run the campaigns. We publish it because we're proud of it, and because our name is on every page.

## What's in the library

This page collects every programmatic advertising resource Genius Monkey has published. A glossary of terms in plain English, a FAQ hub, guides on programmatic, DSPs, CTV, and retail media, platform comparisons, ad spec sheets, whitepapers, and education videos. Each one carries our name because we stand behind it.

## The guides

We helped coin the term programmatic advertising back in 2010. That comes with homework. When the industry asks what a term means, the answer should come from us, so we wrote it down.

### What is programmatic advertising
(links: /resources/guides/what-is-programmatic-advertising)
The automated buying and selling of digital advertising using real-time data and technology. We've been refining that definition since we helped write it. The guide takes it from one line to how a campaign actually gets built.

### What is a DSP
(links: /resources/guides/what-is-a-dsp)
The platform advertisers use to buy ad impressions across exchanges through one interface. Ours is built in-house, so this guide was written next to the machine it describes.

### What is CTV
(links: /resources/guides/what-is-ctv)
Advertising on internet-connected TVs and streaming services, the reach of broadcast with the precision of digital targeting. We've run it since 2019, and the guide covers where it fits in a media plan.

### Retail media, explained
(links: /resources/guides/retail-media)
Our take on what retailer ad networks do well, where they overlap with programmatic, and when they earn a slice of your budget.
