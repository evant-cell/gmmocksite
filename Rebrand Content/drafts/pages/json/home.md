## What Genius Monkey does

Genius Monkey is a programmatic advertising company that plans, buys, and measures digital advertising across display, video, OTT/CTV, audio, and native. Founded in 2009, it runs its own demand-side platform, tracks every campaign from impression to conversion, and pairs the technology with real experts who manage every account.

We helped coin the term programmatic advertising in 2010. We've spent the years since proving the model works. (links: /about)

## Questions buyers actually ask

### Is Genius Monkey a DSP or an agency?
Both, honestly. Genius Monkey owns and operates its own demand-side platform, and a managed team of experts runs it with you. You get the control and reporting of the technology without inheriting a second job, and the service of an agency without the reseller markup.

### How much does programmatic advertising cost?
It depends on channels and budget, but you can see our rates before you talk to anyone. Effective CPMs run $2 to $4 for display, $10 to $22 for video, and $25 to $40 for OTT, all-inclusive, with no long-term contracts. The pricing page has the full breakdown. (links: /pricing)

### What channels does Genius Monkey run?
Display, video, OTT/CTV, audio, and native, all bought programmatically through the same platform and measured in the same dashboard. We added OTT/CTV in 2019, and it is one of the fastest-growing channels we operate in. Most campaigns mix two or three channels around one goal.

### How do I know it's working?
You watch it work. The dashboard shows every placement and every dollar, and attribution reporting ties conversions back to the ads that produced them. onsemi used that reporting to watch cost per conversion fall from $2,000 to under $1. If a campaign isn't working, you'll know that too, and so will we. (links: /platform/measurement-validation)
