## What a programmatic advertising platform is

A programmatic advertising platform buys digital ad space automatically, using real-time data to decide which impressions are worth your money. Genius Monkey runs its own demand-side platform and pairs it with real experts, so campaigns are targeted precisely and measured transparently, then adjusted the moment performance shifts.

Self-serve puts your name on the login and the outcomes on your desk. Managed means the people who built the platform run it with you. Genius Monkey is managed, on technology we own, which is exactly the combination the industry keeps telling you to choose between.

| | Self-serve DSP | Genius Monkey |
|---|---|---|

| Reporting | Whatever you build yourself | Attribution from impression to conversion |

The practical difference is simple. With self-serve, the software is the product and the outcomes are your problem. With managed, the outcomes are the product.

## Questions buyers actually ask

### What is a programmatic advertising platform?
A programmatic advertising platform automates the buying of digital ad space, using real-time bidding and audience data to place ads where they'll perform. Instead of negotiating with individual publishers, you set the goal and the platform buys the impressions that serve it, across display, video, CTV, audio, and native.

### How is a managed platform different from a self-serve DSP?
With a self-serve DSP, your team runs the campaigns and handles the troubleshooting. With a managed platform, experts do that work for you, on technology they know inside out. Genius Monkey is managed. You get the reporting and the control without inheriting a second job.

### Does Genius Monkey run its own DSP?
Yes. Genius Monkey operates its own demand-side platform rather than reselling someone else's. That matters because it removes a layer of markup and a layer of mystery. We built Pixel Monkey, our attribution and performance tracking system, in 2016, and we've been buying programmatically since 2009.

### How much does programmatic advertising cost?
It depends on channels and budget, but you can see our rates before you talk to anyone. Effective CPMs run $2 to $4 for display, $10 to $22 for video, and $25 to $40 for OTT, all-inclusive, with no long-term contracts. The pricing page has the full breakdown. (links: /pricing)

### How do I know it's working?
You watch it work. The dashboard shows every placement and every dollar, and attribution reporting ties conversions back to the ads that produced them. onsemi used that reporting to watch cost per conversion fall from $2,000 to under $1. If a campaign isn't working, you'll know that too, and so will we. (links: /platform/measurement-validation)
