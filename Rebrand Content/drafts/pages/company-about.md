# Who is Genius Monkey

Genius Monkey is the programmatic advertising partner for brands and agencies who are done guessing and want results they can actually see and explain. We run our own demand-side platform, keep reporting transparent, and put a real expert behind every account. No faceless algorithms, no black-box reporting.

[Request a demo]

## Why we're built this way

Our mission is to make programmatic advertising more intelligent, transparent, and client-first. We give brands and agencies of all sizes the expertise and technology to compete with anyone, regardless of budget. We exist to deliver results, not decks.

We grew from scrappy challenger to programmatic leader not by following the playbook, but by rewriting it. We compete with companies that have far bigger war chests, and our answer has never changed. Be sharper, be faster, be more honest than anyone else in the room. That hunger never left.

The vision behind it is simple. A world where every advertising dollar works harder because the technology is smarter and the people running it genuinely care. The right partner changes everything.

We believe advertising should be smarter, more transparent, and more human. Six beliefs run the place.

- Unapologetic leadership. We lead the conversation and set the standard, with confidence backed by performance.
- Built to evolve. We push the platform forward and challenge what everyone assumes is fixed. Staying still is falling behind.
- Proven performance. Bold claims require proof. Every statement we make is supported by results, data, and real experience.
- Confidence with humility. We know we're the best at this, and we never talk down. Our confidence invites conversation.
- People first. Behind the loud brand is a grounded team that treats clients, partners, and teammates with respect and care.
- Do what's right. Every decision around a client's ad spend is based on what's in the client's best interest. Full stop.

## The people behind the platform

Every account has a real expert behind it, someone who treats your budget like it's their own. There is no faceless algorithm here. There are people who care, who pick up the phone, and who would never recommend something that wasn't right for your business.

We treat every client like their only client. We show you the results, explain the uncertainty, and never stop pushing to get you more. That's not something we invented for a pitch.

## The client promise

Genius Monkey was built on one conviction, our clients deserve a partner who puts their best interest first. Every campaign, every dollar, every decision.

Every decision we make around your ad spend is based on one thing. Your best interest. That's not a tagline. It's how we're wired, and it's why clients stay.

## Since 2009

We've been doing this since 2009, long enough to have helped coin the term programmatic advertising in the first place.

| Fact | Detail |
|---|---|
| Founded | 2009, Tempe, Arizona |
| The term | Helped coin "programmatic advertising" in 2010 |
| The platform | Our own DSP, built and run in-house, with real-time optimization and cross-channel attribution |
| Attribution | Pixel Monkey, launched 2016, the industry's first full programmatic attribution and performance tracking system |
| Growth | $2,000,000 investment received in 2015 |
| Channels | Display, video, audio, OTT and CTV (added 2019), social and native display |
| Data partnerships | Experian and Tapad identity graph, plus Stat Social demographic and persona data |

Four things drive every decision here. Unique experiences, the combined expertise of our team and our platform. Outsized value, every client walks away feeling like they got more than they paid for. Trust at scale, consistent results, clear reporting, and a platform partners can count on. Smarter advertising, we remove guesswork and replace it with data.
