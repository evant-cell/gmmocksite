Bring a goal and a budget range. A real expert walks you through the platform, the dashboard, the reporting, built around what you're trying to do. Not a canned deck.

## What you'll see

- The dashboard. Your spend and placements, live, the way clients see them daily.
- The reporting. How attribution shows what actually moved the number, from first impression to conversion.
- Your channels. What a campaign for your goal would look like across the screens your buyers actually use.

## What to bring

A goal, a budget range, and whatever bugs you about your current reporting. That's enough. You don't need a media plan, and you don't need to know the acronyms. That part is our job. If you have reporting you don't trust, bring that too. It makes for a good demo.

## Who you'll talk to

A person, not a sequence. Someone who runs campaigns for a living, who can answer the technical question in the moment instead of circling back, and who will tell you the truth, including when programmatic isn't the answer.
